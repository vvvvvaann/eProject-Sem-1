From the information of the bridges in the world,
our Amazing Bridge has tried to find lots of information about the bridges in the world and choose the bridges:
The most beautiful, the longest, the most dangerous and tall most of all countries in the world. We have successfully found what is needed for the bridges, such as the image, the length, the address, the starting time and the time to rebuild the bridge.
